The World of Fallenborn

Step beyond the veil of known lands and into a realm where nature breathes magic, and history is carved in stone and spirit alike.

The Ancient Forests

Vast, primeval woodlands stretch beyond the horizon, their canopies alive with whispered legends and the silent watch of ancient trees. These forests hide secrets both wondrous and perilous—forgotten relics, ancient beasts, and the lingering echoes of a world long fallen.

Ruins of the Fallen World

Scattered across the land lie the shattered remains of civilizations past, ruins steeped in mystery and power. Here, echoes of a lost age beckon those curious enough to uncover their stories—and daring enough to face the dangers they conceal.

Wildlands and Beyond

Beyond the forests and ruins lie wildlands untamed by man or magic. Jagged mountains, misty swamps, and endless plains stretch to the edges of the map. In these places, the brave find opportunity, danger, and the chance to carve their own destiny.
